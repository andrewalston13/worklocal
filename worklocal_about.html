About Us
Built on the road,
made for the road
How a three-month road trip and a spotty WiFi connection turned into WorkLocal
Andrew & Jenna
Founders of WorkLocal

Hey there! We are Andrew and Jenna, the founders of WorkLocal.

This project started back in 2021 during a three-month road trip through the western United States. We were traveling with an ambitious goal: upload a new travel vlog to YouTube every other day while living on the road.

We could usually edit videos at night from our tent, but uploading them was another story. We needed reliable WiFi, power outlets, and a comfortable place to work for hours at a time.

The problem? Finding a good work spot wasn't easy. We'd show up to a cute local spot that looked perfect, only to realize there was no WiFi, no outlets, or nowhere comfortable to sit and work. This meant we often ended up at big chain coffee shops instead.

The lightbulb moment
That's when the idea for
WorkLocal was born

We wanted to create a resource where remote workers, nomads, and creatives could easily discover work-friendly local spots — all in one place.

Five years later, we finally brought that idea to life. Real WiFi speeds, real outlet ratings, real nomad scores from people who've actually worked there. No chains, no fluff — just the spots worth working from.

Our mission
Help people find great spots to work while supporting the local businesses that make every town unique.
